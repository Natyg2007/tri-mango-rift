Orbit Bloom

A gravity movement prototype focused on slingshot navigation, boost timing, orbiting hazards, and extraction-gate progression.

01

Momentum movement

The player steers a drifting drone with acceleration, drag, boost burn, and gravity wells that can be used for fast routing.

02

Readable hazard design

Orbiting sentinels, mine rings, shield loss, hit cooldowns, and clear telegraphs make the challenge different from the phase games.

03

Objective pacing

Collect every seed to open the extraction gate, then enter a new zone with stronger wells, tighter routes, and faster hazards.

What it demonstrates

3D game systems in the prototype

01

Phase-based mechanics

Matching-color rift blocks teleport the player across randomized arenas. Clearing one color opens a center wormhole and drops into a harder stage from above.

02

3D entity loop

Player movement, random mango spawns, spinning obstacle volumes, paired teleport gates, camera smoothing, scoring, collision checks, and restart flow.

03

Presentation polish

Real-time lighting, depth fog, animated particles, emissive materials, layered HUD, responsive composition, and readable controls.
