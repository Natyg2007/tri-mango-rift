Orbit Bloom

A gravity movement prototype focused on slingshot navigation, boost timing, orbiting hazards, and extraction-gate progression.

01

Momentum movement

The player steers a drifting drone with acceleration, drag, boost burn, and gravity wells that can be used for fast routing.

02

03

Project overview

Three playable prototypes with different gameplay systems.

This standalone HTML/CSS/JavaScript project includes one Three.js 3D game and two separate 2D canvas prototypes. It is designed to show gameplay implementation, engine thinking, visual polish, and multiple playable mechanics instead of a static page.

  • Custom input, collision, scoring, timer, and restart systems.
  • Randomized arenas, progression rules, hazards, and objective loops.
  • Responsive HUDs and polished presentation for desktop and mobile.
